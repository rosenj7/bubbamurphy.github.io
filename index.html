Experiment 001: Deep Roasted Lobster Oil

Experiment 002: Lobster "Pesto" Butter (The Recovery)

Status: Success Zero Waste

The Hypothesis:

The "sludge" trapped in the filter from Exp 001 contains ~230ml of lost oil and high-surface-area shell dust. Mixing this with dairy fat should recover the flavor.

The Protocol:

  • Input A: ~230ml Lobster Sludge (Oil-saturated shell dust)
  • Input B: 2 Sticks Unsalted Butter (225g)
  • Method: Folded sludge into softened butter.
  • Ratio: ~1:1 (Volume/Weight)

Sensory Results:

Deep orange/brown color. Intensity is higher than the oil alone due to shell solids. Texture is softer than standard butter (due to oil content). Freezing required for storage.
