## Prep Notes
Bay scallops are delicate and cure quickly. Ensure they are thoroughly dried before slicing.

## Final Ratio Summary
* **Bay Scallops:** 100g (Base)
* **Lime Juice:** 25g (25% Acid Ratio)
* **High-Quality Olive Oil:** 10g
* **Salt:** 1.5g (1.5% w/w)

## Method
1. Dice scallops into 1/2 cm pieces.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice and salt until dissolved.
3. Gently fold the scallops into the curing liquid just before serving (cure time is minimal, approx. 5 minutes).
4. Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with finely minced tarragon.
